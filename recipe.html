Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon mirchi powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon Garam masala
  • 1/2 tablespoon turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion and fry until light golden.
  3. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble well.
  4. Add cooked rice
  5. Add salt, mirchi powder, Garam masala, turmeric powder and mix well..
